JOB SPHERE ANALYSIS


ABOUT

A job analysis dashboard is a visual representation of key metrics and insights derived from the process of job analysis within an organization. It provides a dynamic and comprehensive overview of various job-related factors, including job roles, responsibilities, skill requirements, and performance indicators. This tool enables HR professionals and decision-makers to assess workforce needs, identify skill gaps, and streamline recruitment, training, and talent management strategies. Through inte Through interactive charts and graphs, the job analysis dashboard offers a user-friendly interface for stakeholders to make data-driven decisions, ensuring alignment between organizational goals and the skills and competencies of the workforce.
